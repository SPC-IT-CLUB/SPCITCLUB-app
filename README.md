# SPCITCLUB App
The SPC IT CLUB APP For Mobile

Install Our App
iPhone / iPad

Open this website in Safari
Tap the Share button (⬆️) at the bottom
Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
Tap "Add"

Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen.

Android

Open this website in Chrome
Tap "Install" when prompted

Or tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home screen"


Tap "Install" to confirm

Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen.

Note: Must use Safari on iPhone/iPad, and Chrome works best on Android.
